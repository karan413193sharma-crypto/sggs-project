Why Choose MA Music at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur?

MA Music equips students with advanced knowledge in Indian classical music, vocal, instrumental, and music theory, combined with practical performance experience and research skills.

  • Comprehensive training in vocal and instrumental music
  • Focus on theory, history, and performance skills
  • Opportunities for recitals, concerts, and research projects
  • Preparation for careers in teaching, performance, and cultural organizations
MA Music – Syllabus

Semester 1

  • Vocal Training I
  • Music Theory & Notation
  • Indian Classical Music History

Semester 2

  • Instrumental Training I
  • Composition & Arrangement
  • Music Research Methodology

Semester 3

  • Vocal & Instrumental Advanced Techniques
  • Musicology & Ethnomusicology
  • Elective Paper I

Semester 4

  • Recitals & Performance Project
  • Music Criticism & Research Paper
  • Elective Paper II
Program Outcomes
  • Advanced knowledge of music theory and practice
  • Performance and research skills development
  • Preparedness for teaching, performance, and cultural roles
  • Foundation for higher studies – M.Phil, Ph.D.
