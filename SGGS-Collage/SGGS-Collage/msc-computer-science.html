M.Sc. Computer Science – Overview

M.Sc. Computer Science is a 2-year postgraduate program (4 semesters) designed for students interested in Advanced Programming, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Software Development.

Why Choose M.Sc. CS?

  • Hands-on exposure to programming, databases, and software tools
  • Develop analytical and problem-solving skills for IT industry
  • Opportunities for research projects, internships, and competitive exams
  • Prepares for higher studies like Ph.D., M.Tech, or IT certifications

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Software Developers, Data Analysts, AI/ML Engineers, Cloud Specialists, System Analysts, or pursue higher studies.

Admission

Admissions for M.Sc. Computer Science are open! Eligible students (B.Sc. Computer Science/IT/Maths/CS with minimum 45% marks) can apply online.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The M.Sc. Computer Science program is a 2-year postgraduate course divided into 4 semesters.

Duration
