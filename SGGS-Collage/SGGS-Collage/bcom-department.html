Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for B.Com?
10+2 in Commerce or equivalent stream from a recognized board.

Yes, including CA, CMA, CS, and CFP along with the B.Com curriculum.
Is internship mandatory?
Yes, students are encouraged to complete internships in finance, accounting, or management.
