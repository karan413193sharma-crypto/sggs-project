B.Sc. Non-Medical – Overview

B.Sc. Non-Medical is a 3-year undergraduate program (6 semesters) designed for students passionate about Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Computer Science. This course prepares students for a wide range of careers in science, technology, and research.

Why Choose B.Sc. Non-Medical?

  • Hands-on experience in modern labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Computer Science.
  • Develops analytical, computational, and problem-solving skills for modern industries.
  • Opportunities for research projects and competitive exams: NDA, IIT-JAM, SSC.
  • Prepares for higher studies such as M.Sc., MCA, MBA, B.Ed., and more.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as Scientists, Research Assistants, IT Developers, Data Analysts, Lecturers, Defence Officers, or Quality Control Analysts.

Admission

Admissions for B.Sc. Non-Medical are open! Eligible students (10+2 Science, min 35% marks) can apply online. Secure your seat and start your journey in science and technology.

B.Sc. Non-Medical – Detailed Syllabus

The B.Sc. Non-Medical program is a 3-year undergraduate course (6 semesters) designed for students interested in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Computer Science. Below is the detailed syllabus for each semester.

  • DSC-1: Physics
  • DSC-2: Mathematics
  • DSC-3: Chemistry or Computer Science & Applications
  • MDC/IDC: Geography & Environment
