Why Choose M.Sc. Fashion Designing at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur?

M.Sc. Fashion Designing is a postgraduate program focusing on advanced fashion theory, research, textile innovation, design management, and professional practice in the fashion industry.

  • Advanced Fashion & Textile Research Labs
  • Specialized Design & Innovation Workshops
  • Industry Projects & Portfolio Expansion
  • Exposure to Global Fashion Trends
  • Opportunities in Fashion Management & Entrepreneurship
  • Highly Experienced Faculty

M.Sc. Fashion Designing – Overview

The 2-year program (4 semesters) equips students with advanced knowledge in fashion theory, textile science, garment construction, CAD, research, and professional practice.

Why Choose M.Sc. Fashion Designing?

  • Advanced workshops in pattern making, draping, and garment innovation.
  • Research-based projects and portfolio development.
  • Industry exposure through internships and collaborations.
  • Prepares students for leadership, entrepreneurship, and higher education.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Fashion Researchers, Textile Innovators, Fashion Entrepreneurs, CAD Specialists, Fashion Managers, or Stylists.

Admission

Admissions for M.Sc. Fashion Designing are open! Eligible students (B.Sc. Fashion Designing or equivalent) can apply online to enhance their creative and research skills in fashion design.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The M.Sc. Fashion Designing program is a 2-year postgraduate course divided into 4 semesters, focusing on advanced research, professional practice, and design innovation.

Duration

2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility

B.Sc. Fashion Designing or Equivalent Degree with minimum 50% marks

Program Outcomes
  • Acquire advanced skills in fashion design, research, and textile innovation.
  • Develop professional portfolios and research projects.
  • Gain expertise in digital fashion tools, CAD, and garment construction.
  • Prepare for leadership roles in fashion industry, academia, or entrepreneurship.
  • Opportunities to participate in global fashion events, workshops, and collaborations.

M.Sc. Fashion Designing – Detailed Syllabus

The syllabus includes advanced topics in Fashion, Textile, CAD, Research, and Professional Practice across 4 semesters.

  • Advanced Fashion Design Theory
  • Textile & Material Innovation
  • Garment Construction & Draping Techniques
  • Digital Fashion Design Tools
  • Research Methodology in Fashion
  • Design Management & Entrepreneurship
  • Portfolio Development & Presentation
  • Trend Forecasting & Styling
  • Fashion Marketing & Merchandising
  • Advanced CAD Applications
  • Research Project / Case Study
  • Advanced Garment Innovation & Production Techniques
  • Professional Practice & Internship
  • Final Portfolio & Industry Presentation
  • Entrepreneurship & Fashion Business Management
  • Global Fashion Trends & Innovation
  • Dissertation / Capstone Project
