Why Choose M.Sc. Mathematics?

M.Sc. Mathematics at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur equips students with advanced knowledge in pure and applied mathematics, fostering analytical, computational, and research skills required for modern careers.

  • Experienced Faculty & Research-Oriented Teaching
  • Advanced Mathematical Lab & Computational Tools
  • Preparation for Competitive Exams: NET, GATE, Civil Services
  • Strong Foundation for Ph.D. & Higher Studies
  • Career Opportunities in Teaching, Data Analysis, and Research

M.Sc. Mathematics – Overview

The M.Sc. Mathematics program is a 2-year postgraduate course (4 semesters) focused on advanced topics in algebra, analysis, statistics, and applied mathematics. Students learn through a mix of theoretical and practical sessions.

Career Opportunities: Graduates can work as Academicians, Data Analysts, Statisticians, Research Scientists, or pursue Ph.D.

Admission

Admissions for M.Sc. Mathematics are open for students with a B.Sc. in Mathematics or related fields with minimum 50% marks.

Duration
