About S.G.G.S

Sri Guru Gobind Singh Khalsa College, Mahilpur, Distt. Hoshiarpur (Punjab), India

Sri Guru Gobind Singh Khalsa College, Mahilpur stands out as one of Punjab’s most trusted institutions for quality education and career-focused learning. With decades of impact, the college has built a strong reputation for academic excellence, discipline, and real-world student development. What makes it special is its ability to blend traditional values with modern teaching methods.

It is a matter of great honour that its Football Team (Men) won the Panjab University ‘A’ Division Football Championship for 20 years continuously. The Women’s team has also excelled. NCC cadet Rohit Kumar led the Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, and UT Chandigarh Directorate in the Republic Day Parade 2006.

With a strong faculty, well-equipped library (50,000+ books), and two modern computer labs, the college ensures students get a future-ready education. It’s a place where potential turns into performance.

Our Inspiration

PRINCIPAL HARBHAJAN SINGH

PRINCIPAL HARBHAJAN SINGH

Principal Harbhajan Singh, the founding leader of Sri Guru Gobind Singh Khalsa College, Mahilpur, was a visionary who invested his entire life and resources into creating a reputed centre of learning. Long before independence, he imagined a prestigious co-educational college—and achieved it. Under his leadership, the institution gained national fame in football, earning the title “FOOTBALL NURSERY OF ASIA”.

His legacy opened doors for generations in the armed forces, civil services, sports and major government sectors. His values of truth, discipline and hard work still inspire students today.

Sant Baba Hari Singh Memorial Khalsa College of Education, Mahilpur, stands today as a flourishing branch of the noble tree he planted 68 years ago, proudly following in the footsteps of this legendary personality.

RIGHT TO INFORMATION ACT – 2005

SSri Guru Gobind Singh Khalsa College, Mahilpur (Hoshiarpur, Punjab), is one of Northern India’s oldest and most respected rural colleges. Established in 1946 by Sant Baba Hari Singh Ji Kaharpuri, it was founded to provide quality higher education to students from rural, backward and kandi areas.

The college maintains strict transparency. The Right to Information Act, 2005 is fully implemented, allowing citizens to request information about its functioning.The Principal acts as the Information Officer, supported by an Assistant Public Information Officer to ensure efficient information access.
