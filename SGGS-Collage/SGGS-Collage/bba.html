BBA – Overview

BBA is a 3-year undergraduate program (6 semesters) designed for students interested in Management, Marketing, Human Resource, and Entrepreneurship.

Why Choose BBA?

  • Hands-on exposure to management tools, marketing, and HR software
  • Develop leadership, analytical, and decision-making skills
  • Opportunities for internships, workshops, and corporate exposure
  • Prepares for higher studies like MBA, PGDM, or entrepreneurship ventures

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Business Managers, HR Executives, Marketing Professionals, Entrepreneurs, Business Analysts, or pursue higher studies.

Admission

Admissions for BBA are open! Eligible students (10+2 with any stream, minimum 35% marks) can apply online.
