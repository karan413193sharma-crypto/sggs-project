Why Choose M.P.Ed at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur?

M.P.Ed at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur provides advanced training in physical education, sports science, fitness management, and research in sports performance. Students get practical experience through labs, internships, and research projects.

M.P.Ed – Overview

M.P.Ed is a 2-year postgraduate program (4 semesters) designed for students interested in Physical Education, Sports Science, Fitness Training, and Coaching.

Why Choose M.P.Ed?

  • Hands-on exposure to fitness labs, sports training, and coaching techniques
  • Develop expertise in sports science, physiology, and performance analysis
  • Opportunities for research projects, internships, and competitive coaching exams
  • Prepares for higher studies like Ph.D. in Physical Education or Sports Management

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Physical Education Teachers, Sports Coaches, Fitness Trainers, Sports Scientists, Physiotherapists, or pursue higher studies and research in sports and fitness domains.

Admission

Admissions for M.P.Ed are open! Eligible students (B.P.Ed graduates, min 50% marks) can apply online.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The M.P.Ed program is a 2-year postgraduate course divided into 4 semesters.

Duration

2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility

B.P.Ed or equivalent with minimum 50% marks

Program Outcomes
  • Develop advanced physical education and sports coaching skills
  • Hands-on experience in fitness labs and sports training
  • Ability to analyze physical performance and design training programs
  • Preparation for internships, research, and coaching certifications
  • Enhance leadership, communication, and team management skills
