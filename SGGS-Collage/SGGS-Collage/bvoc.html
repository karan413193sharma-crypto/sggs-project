B.Voc – Overview

B.Voc is a 3-year undergraduate program focused on vocational skills, practical training, and industry-oriented knowledge to make students job-ready.

Why Choose B.Voc?

  • Hands-on training in industry-relevant skills
  • Develop professional, managerial, and technical skills
  • Opportunities for internships, projects, and industrial visits
  • Prepares for employment, entrepreneurship, or higher vocational studies

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as skilled professionals, project associates, industry trainees, or entrepreneurs in their chosen vocational domain.

Admission

Admissions for B.Voc are open! Eligible students (10+2 pass in relevant streams) can apply online.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The B.Voc program is a 3-year course divided into 6 semesters.

Duration

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility

10+2 Pass in any relevant stream from a recognized board

Program Outcomes
  • Acquire vocational skills aligned with industry needs
  • Gain hands-on experience through projects and practical sessions
  • Develop professional and technical competencies
  • Enhance employability and entrepreneurship capabilities
  • Prepare for higher studies or specialized vocational courses
