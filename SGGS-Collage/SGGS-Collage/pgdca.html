Why Choose PGDCA at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur?

PGDCA at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur is a one-year postgraduate diploma that equips students with comprehensive knowledge in computer applications, programming, networking, and software development. The program emphasizes hands-on projects, skill development, and industry-oriented training.

  • Comprehensive Computer Applications Curriculum

  • Preparation for IT Certifications & Higher Studies

PGDCA – Overview

PGDCA is a 1-year program (2 semesters) designed for students seeking to develop skills in computer applications, programming, web technologies, and networking.

Why Choose PGDCA?

  • Hands-on experience in programming, databases, and web development
  • Industry-oriented projects and practical assignments
  • Prepares students for IT jobs and software careers
  • Skill enhancement for higher studies or certifications in IT

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Software Developers, Database Administrators, Network Administrators, Web Developers, IT Support Specialists, or pursue further certifications in technologies like Java, Python, .NET, Networking, and Cybersecurity.

Admission

Admissions for PGDCA are open! Eligible students (Graduates in any discipline) can apply online.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The PGDCA program is a 1-year diploma divided into 2 semesters.
