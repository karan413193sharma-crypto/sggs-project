Why Choose MA Economics at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur?

MA Economics provides advanced knowledge in economic theory, quantitative methods, and applied economics, preparing students for research, teaching, and policy roles.

  • Advanced curriculum covering Micro, Macro, and Econometrics
  • Research projects & applied assignments
  • Skill development in analytical and quantitative methods
  • Opportunities for higher studies, research, and policy roles

MA Economics – Overview

The 2-year postgraduate program (4 semesters) focuses on analytical and research skills in economics, preparing students for careers in academia, government, research organizations, and finance.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue roles in Research, Teaching, Government & Policy, Banking & Finance, or higher studies like M.Phil, Ph.D., or competitive exams.

MA Economics – Syllabus

Semester 1

  • Microeconomic Theory I
  • Macroeconomic Theory I
  • Mathematical Methods for Economics

Semester 2

  • Microeconomic Theory II
  • Macroeconomic Theory II
  • Econometrics I

Semester 3

  • Development Economics
  • Public Finance & Policy
  • Econometrics II

Semester 4

  • International Economics
  • Research Project / Dissertation
  • Elective Papers
