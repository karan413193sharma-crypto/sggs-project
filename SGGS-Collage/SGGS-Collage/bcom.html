B.Com – Overview

B.Com is a 3-year undergraduate program (6 semesters) designed for students interested in Accounting, Economics, Business Studies and Finance.

Why Choose B.Com?

  • Hands-on exposure to accounting, finance, and business software
  • Develop analytical and business decision-making skills
  • Opportunities for research projects, internships, and competitive exams
  • Prepares for higher studies like M.Com, MBA, CA, CS, ICWA

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Accountants, Business Analysts, Tax Consultants, Auditors, Bank Officers, Entrepreneurs, or pursue higher studies.

Admission

Admissions for B.Com are open! Eligible students (10+2 with Commerce/Maths/Economics, min 35% marks) can apply online.
