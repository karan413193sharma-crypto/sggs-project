Why Choose B.Sc. Medical at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur?

B.Sc. Medical is designed for students with a passion for Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, offering hands-on lab work, modern facilities, experienced faculty, and strong career prospects in science, healthcare, and research sectors.

  • Modern Biology, Chemistry & Physics Labs
  • Preparation for Competitive Exams: NEET, CSIR NET, IIT-JAM

  • Strong Career & Higher Education Opportunities

B.Sc. Medical – Overview

B.Sc. Medical is a 3-year undergraduate program (6 semesters) for students interested in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and allied sciences. The course prepares students for careers in research, healthcare, biotechnology, and higher education.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue roles in Healthcare, Research Labs, Biotechnology, Education, Pharmaceutical Industry, or higher studies like M.Sc., MBBS, B.Ed., or CSIR NET.
