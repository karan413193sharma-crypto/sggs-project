Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for MA?
A Bachelor's degree with minimum 45% (or as per college policy). Specific subject requirements depend on specialization.
Are scholarships available?
Yes — merit and need-based scholarships are offered. See admissions for details.
Is there an entrance exam?
Some specializations may require an entrance test; others use merit and interview-based selection.
