Why Choose BA / BA (Hons.) at SGGS Khalsa College Mahilpur?

BA / BA (Hons.) equips students with analytical, research, and communication skills in subjects like History, Political Science, Literature. The program emphasizes critical thinking, cultural awareness, and practical knowledge through projects and assignments.

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering Humanities & Social Sciences
  • Research Projects & Practical Assignments
  • Skill development in communication and analysis
  • Opportunities for higher studies & competitive exams

BA / BA (Hons.) – Overview

The 3-year undergraduate program (6 semesters) focuses on Humanities and Social Sciences disciplines, developing critical thinking, research, and academic skills. It prepares students for careers in education, civil services, research, and cultural organizations.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue roles in Teaching, Research, Journalism, Civil Services, Cultural Organizations, or higher studies like M.A., M.Phil, Ph.D., or competitive exams.

BA / BA (Hons.) – Syllabus

Semester 1

  • History: Ancient Civilizations
  • Political Science: Introduction to Political Theory
  • English Literature: Foundations of Literature

Semester 2

  • History: Medieval India
  • Political Science: Indian Constitution
  • English Literature: Poetry and Prose

Semester 3

  • History: Modern India
  • Political Science: Comparative Politics
  • English Literature: Drama and Fiction

Semester 4

  • History: World History
  • Political Science: International Relations
  • English Literature: Literary Criticism

Semester 5

  • History: Contemporary India
  • Political Science: Public Administration
  • English Literature: Modern Literature

Semester 6

  • History: Research Project
  • Political Science: Research Project
  • English Literature: Dissertation / Project Work
