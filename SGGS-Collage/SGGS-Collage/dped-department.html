Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility criteria?
Candidates should have completed 10+2 / Intermediate in any stream.
Is practical training included?
Yes, intensive practical sessions in sports and fitness are mandatory.
Can I pursue teaching after DPED?
Yes, DPED graduates are eligible for teaching physical education in schools and academies.
