M.Sc. Physics – Overview

M.Sc. Physics is a 2-year postgraduate program (4 semesters) designed for students passionate about advanced Physics topics, research, and applications in technology and industry.

Why Choose M.Sc. Physics?

  • Hands-on experience in modern Physics labs.
  • Develops analytical, computational, and research skills.
  • Opportunities for research projects and competitive exams: NET, GATE.
  • Prepares for higher studies like Ph.D. and specialized courses.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as Physicists, Research Scientists, Data Analysts, Lecturers, Defence Scientists, or pursue further studies like Ph.D.

Admission

Admissions for M.Sc. Physics are open! Eligible students (B.Sc. Physics/Non-Medical Science, minimum 50% marks) can apply online.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The M.Sc. Physics program is a 2-year postgraduate course divided into 4 semesters.

Duration

2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility

B.Sc. Physics / Non-Medical Science with minimum 50% marks

Program Outcomes
  • Develop advanced analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Gain hands-on experience in Physics laboratories.
  • Build scientific thinking, innovation ability, and research skills.
  • Prepare for NET, GATE, and higher education opportunities.
  • Enhance teamwork, communication, and technical skills.
