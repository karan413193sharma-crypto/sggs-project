Academic Requirements

Passed 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board with minimum required percentage in relevant subjects.

  • For BA/B.Sc/B.Com: 10+2 with 45% aggregate
  • For BCA: 10+2 with Mathematics and 50% aggregate
  • For M.Sc: Bachelor degree with 50% marks in relevant subject

Document Verification

All candidates must submit original documents for verification during the admission process.

  • Matriculation certificate (Date of Birth proof)
  • Character certificate from previous institution
  • Detailed marks card and provisional certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)

Entrance Requirements

Certain courses require entrance test qualification as per university guidelines.

  • M.Sc Physics and Chemistry: OCET entrance test
  • Minimum qualifying marks in entrance exam
  • Valid entrance test scorecard required
  • Merit-based selection process

Important Note

All admissions are subject to verification and approval by Panjab University, Chandigarh. The college reserves the right to refuse or terminate any admission without assigning reasons. Students admitted on wrong information will face immediate cancellation of admission and disciplinary action.
